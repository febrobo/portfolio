Bike Anti-Lock Braking Systems Performance Across Varied Road Conditions

Nov 2023 - Dec 2023

Python MATLAB Control Systems Simulation

Overview

Studied ABS performance in different weather conditions across varied road surfaces and made a simulation model of various control methods. Combined two different controller approaches and made a controller model to have a dynamic and real-time performance.

Technical Approach

  • Analyzed ABS behavior under various weather and road conditions
  • Developed simulation models for different control algorithms
  • Integrated multiple control strategies for optimal performance
  • Validated controller effectiveness through simulation testing
  • Achieved dynamic real-time braking control across diverse conditions

Results

Successfully created a hybrid controller that demonstrated improved braking performance across wet, dry, and varying road surface conditions. The simulation validated the effectiveness of the combined control approach in maintaining vehicle stability and reducing stopping distances.
