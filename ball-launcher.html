Design, Simulation & Development of Ball Launcher

Aug 2020 - May 2022

Mechanical Design Fusion 360 Prototyping Manufacturing

Overview

Re-designed and re-developed Ball Launcher Machines with current technology in such a way that they are affordable for all without compromising on the areas of practice a player needs. Fabricated a fully functional tennis ball launcher and received an O grade as well as commendation from the Department.

Design & Development

  • Redesigned ball launcher mechanism for cost-effective manufacturing
  • Utilized Fusion 360 for CAD modeling and simulation
  • Integrated modern components while maintaining affordability
  • Fabricated fully functional prototype
  • Conducted performance testing and validation
  • Optimized for multiple practice scenarios and player needs

Results & Recognition

The project successfully demonstrated that high-quality training equipment can be made accessible and affordable. The fully functional tennis ball launcher received an O grade (Outstanding) and special commendation from the Department for its innovation and practical application.

Skills Demonstrated

Communication, Ansys Mechanical FEA Software, Creative Problem Solving, Variety of Audiences, Autodesk Fusion 360, Microsoft Word, Research Skills, Academic Research
